# Managing Subscriptions and User Accounts

Cinema8 offers flexible subscription plans to meet different needs, whether you're an individual creator or part of a large team. This guide explains how to manage your subscription, upgrade or downgrade plans, and control user accounts.

## How to Manage Your Cinema8 Subscription

<Steps>

### Access your subscription settings  
Log in to Cinema8 and click on your profile in the top-right corner. Select **Account Settings > Subscription & Billing**.

### View your current plan  
The dashboard will display your current plan, billing cycle, and available features.

### Upgrade or downgrade your plan  
Click **Change Plan** to view available options. Select the plan that fits your needs and confirm the upgrade or downgrade.  
If upgrading, your new features will be activated immediately.

### Update payment details  
Click **Manage Payment Method** to update your credit card or billing information.

### Cancel or pause your subscription  
Go to **Subscription Settings** and select the appropriate option.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Feature Availability Note">
Some features, like AI-powered subtitles, are only available on paid plans.  
If you need access, consider upgrading your plan.
</Callout>

## Managing User Accounts in Cinema8

If you have a team plan, you can manage multiple user accounts within your subscription. Go to **Account Settings > Users**.

- **View all users:** View all users currently on your account.  
- **Add new users:** Click **Invite User** and enter their email. Assign a role (Admin, Editor, Viewer).
