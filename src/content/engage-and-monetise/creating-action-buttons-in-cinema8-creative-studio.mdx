# Creating Action Buttons in Cinema8 Creative Studio

Action buttons are interactive elements that prompt viewers to engage with your content‚Äîwhether it‚Äôs clicking a link, subscribing, or downloading a file. ## How to Create an Action Button

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open the Video in Creative Studio

- Log into your **Cinema8 dashboard**
- Select your project and click **Edit** to open it in Creative Studio

### Step 2: Add a Button Widget

- In the **Elements Panel**, drag the **Widget Element** onto the video timeline
- From the **Widget Library**, choose a button type:
  - **Call-to-Action Button**
  - **Download Button**
  - **Shop Now Button**

### Step 3: Customize Button Properties

- Select the button on your timeline to open the **Properties Panel**
- Set the following:
  - **Text** ‚Äì Use a clear action label (e.g., "Learn More", "Sign Up Now")
  - **Link** ‚Äì Add the destination URL or set a trigger action
  - **Timing** ‚Äì Choose when the button appears and disappears
  - **Positioning** ‚Äì Adjust placement using top, left, width, and height controls

### Step 4: Style the Button

- Adjust the visual appearance to match your branding:
  - Font size and color
  - Background color or gradient
  - Border and shadow effects
  - Hover interactions (e.g., color change, animation)

### Step 5: Add Entry and Exit Animations

- Choose animations in the **Properties Panel**:
  - Options: Fade, Slide, Bounce, Zoom, etc.
- Adjust duration and delay to sync with your video flow

### Step 6: Preview and Save

- Use **Preview Mode** to check placement and behavior
- Click **Save** once everything looks and works as expected

</Steps>
