# Creating Branching Video Experiences

## How to Use Storyflow to Build Personalized Video Journeys

**Design Video Branching** is a powerful feature in Cinema8 that allows you to connect multiple interactive videos into a seamless story. Using questions and logic, you can direct viewers to different paths based on their choices â€” perfect for learning, onboarding, or marketing use cases.

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Media Library

Click the **Media Library** button in the left panel to browse the videos you've uploaded previously.

![Media Library](/screenshot/advanced-interactivity/branching-experiences-1.webp)

---

### Step 2: Create a New Interactive Video

Click the **New Interactive Video** button to start a fresh project for your branching experience.

---

### Step 3: Edit Video Info

Use the **Edit Video Info** button to name your video, set a description, and adjust any project-level settings.

---

### Step 4: Launch the Branching Editor

Click the **Design Video Branching** button to open the Storyflow editor. This is where you can visually connect different video nodes using drag-and-drop.

![Branching Editor](/screenshot/advanced-interactivity/branching-experiences-2.webp)

</Steps>

Once you've created your Storyflow structure, you can start inserting **questions** or **conditional paths** to guide the viewer.
