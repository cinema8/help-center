# Creating New Interactive Live Stream

<Callout type="important">
To broadcast interactively via Cinema8, start by creating a new live stream using the <strong>Create Live Project</strong> interface.
</Callout>

## How to Set Up an Interactive Live Broadcast in Cinema8

<Steps>

### Step 1: Enter Basic Information

In the <strong>Project Information</strong> section:

- <strong>Name</strong>: Provide a clear and descriptive title for your live broadcast.
- <strong>Description</strong>: Summarize the purpose or content of your stream.
- <strong>Tags</strong>: Add relevant keywords for categorization and easier discovery.

### Step 2: Customize Project Settings

- <strong>Project Media</strong>: On the <em>Projects</em> screen, choose a thumbnail or preview image for the live stream.
- <strong>Privacy Settings</strong>: Decide who can view the stream:
  - <strong>Public</strong>: Anyone can access it.
  - <strong>Private</strong>: Limited to a defined audience.
  - <strong>Unlisted</strong>: Not publicly listed or searchable.

- <strong>Folder</strong>: Select a destination folder where the completed stream will be stored.

### Step 3: Adjust Display Settings

Set your display preferences for mobile compatibility:

- Choose between <strong>Portrait</strong> or <strong>Landscape</strong> mode.
- Be aware that certain devices may crop the video depending on orientation.

### Step 4: Create and Retrieve RTMP Info

Once all details are filled in, click <strong>Create</strong>.

- The <strong>RTMP Information</strong> section will now be visible.
- You will receive your <strong>RTMP Address</strong> and <strong>Stream Name</strong>.

Use these credentials in your broadcasting software to initiate the live stream.

</Steps>
