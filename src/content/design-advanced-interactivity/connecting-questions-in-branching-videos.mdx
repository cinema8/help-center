# Connecting Questions in Branching Videos

## Creating Dynamic Viewer Experiences with Interactive Video Branching in Cinema8

When creating interactive videos in Cinema8, you can link multiple videos together using the **Storyflow** feature. By inserting questions between segments, you give viewers control over the path they take—making each experience uniquely personal.

<Callout type="info">
Use branching to guide viewers through customized scenarios based on their answers. Ideal for training, e-learning, and marketing journeys.
</Callout>

---

## How to Connect Questions Between Videos

<Steps>

### Step 1: Access Storyflow Editor  
Hover over your interactive video in the dashboard and click the three-dot menu. Select **Quick-Start Dashboard** or go directly to **Design Video Branching**.

### Step 2: Edit Video Details (Optional)  
Click on **Edit Video Info** to update project metadata like name, description, or thumbnail.

### Step 3: Open the Branching Designer  
Use the **Design Video Branching** button to launch the visual editor. Here, you’ll see all videos and how they’re connected.

### Step 4: Add Questions as Decision Points  
Click the **“+” button** on a video node and choose **Question**. You can create:
- Single-select or multi-select questions
- Define answers and connect each one to different video segments

### Step 5: Configure Branching Logic  
Connect answers to the relevant next videos. These connections determine how viewers navigate through the content.

### Step 6: Use Navigation Tools  
Inside the editor, you can:
- Zoom in/out or fullscreen the canvas  
- Lock elements in place  
- Change the line style between segments  
- Use the **Preview** button to simulate the full branching experience

</Steps>
