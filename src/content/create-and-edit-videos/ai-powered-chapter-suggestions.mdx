## Why Use AI-Powered Chapters?

- **Save time** by letting AI detect key moments in your video.
- **Improve viewer engagement** through quick access to relevant sections.
- **Leverage intelligent analysis** that understands content flow for optimal chapter placement.

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Your Video in the Studio

- Go to **My Videos** in your dashboard.
- Select a video and click **Edit Video**.
- Navigate to the **Chapters** section.

### Step 2: Generate AI-Based Chapters

- Click the **Generate with AI** button.
- Wait while AI processes your video to identify major sections.
- Suggested chapters will appear along the timeline.

### Step 3: Review and Customize

- Edit chapter titles and adjust timestamps as needed.
- Add or remove chapters manually to better match your narrative.
- Align chapters with key transitions or content shifts.

</Steps>

## Optimizing Your Chapters

- **Fine-tune manually** for clarity and accuracy.
- **Use engagement data** to align chapters with audience interests.
- **Preview your video** to ensure smooth chapter transitions before publishing.
