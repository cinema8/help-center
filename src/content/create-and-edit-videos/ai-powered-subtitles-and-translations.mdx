# AI-Powered Subtitles and Translations  
<span className="block text-gray-500 text-base font-normal mt-1">Create inclusive, multilingual videos with AI</span>

Cinema8 simplifies subtitle creation by using AI to **automatically generate and translate subtitles in over 100 languages**.  
This powerful feature enhances accessibility and global engagement — allowing your content to resonate with diverse audiences effortlessly.

---

## <span className="inline-flex items-center gap-2 text-gray-900 dark:text-white"><SparklesIcon className="w-5 h-5 text-blue-600" /> How to Generate AI Subtitles</span>

<Steps>
### Step 1: Enable AI Subtitles

- Go to **My Videos** on the Cinema8 dashboard.  
- Select a video and click **"Edit Video"**.  
- Open the **Subtitle Editor** and click **"Let AI Suggest Subtitles"**.

### Step 2: AI Subtitle Processing

- The AI analyzes your video and generates subtitles in the original language.  
- It runs in the background — processing time varies with video length.  
- Once ready, you can fine-tune timing and text manually in the editor.
</Steps>

---

## <span className="inline-flex items-center gap-2 text-gray-900 dark:text-white"><LanguageIcon className="w-5 h-5 text-blue-600" /> How to Translate Subtitles with AI</span>

<Steps>
### Step 1: Select a Target Language

- Inside the **Subtitle Editor**, click **"Translate Subtitles"**.  
- Choose from over **100 available languages**.

### Step 2: AI Translation Processing

- AI will automatically translate the existing subtitle track.  
- Review the output and make edits as needed for tone or accuracy.
</Steps>

## <span className="inline-flex items-center gap-2 text-gray-900 dark:text-white"><AdjustmentsHorizontalIcon className="w-5 h-5 text-blue-600" /> Pro Tips for Better Results</span>

- **Multi-Language Support:** Add multiple subtitle tracks to serve international audiences.  
- **Edit After AI:** Manual adjustments improve clarity, tone, and sync.  
- **Feature Access:** AI-powered subtitles are available in **Starter plans and above**.
