# Creating Your First Video

Cinema8 makes it easy to go from idea to a fully interactive, high-quality video — whether it’s a tutorial, a product demo, or an immersive 360° experience.  
This guide walks you through the full workflow to help you confidently publish your first video.

---

<Steps>

### Step 1: Start a New Video

- Log in to your **Cinema8 Dashboard**.
- Click the **“New Video”** button.
- Choose how you want to begin:
  - **Upload an existing video**
  - **Record your screen**
  - **Use a ready-made template**
  - **Create a 360° interactive video**

<Callout type="info">
Not sure where to start? Try a template to explore interactive layouts and speed up production.
</Callout>

---

### Step 2: Add Interactivity

Make your content more engaging with built-in interaction tools:

- Click **“Add Interactivity”** in the editor.
- Drag and drop elements such as:
  - Clickable **CTAs**
  - Embedded **forms**
  - **Hotspots**
  - Interactive **quizzes**

These elements let your audience engage directly with your content — boosting retention and ROI.

---

### Step 3: Edit Your Video

Use Cinema8’s built-in tools to polish your project:

- Trim and cut clips to improve pacing.
- Add **background music** and **sound effects** for a polished feel.
- Adjust transitions and layer elements on the timeline.

<Callout type="important">
✨ Tip: Preview each scene after trimming to ensure that your interactions sync properly.
</Callout>

---

### Step 4: Preview and Publish

Once you’re happy with your edits:

1. Click **“Preview”** to test all interactions.
2. Click **“Publish”** to go live.
3. Choose your video’s visibility:
   - **Public**: Accessible to everyone
   - **Private**: Restricted to selected users
   - **Password Protected**: Adds an extra layer of security

Cinema8 also lets you generate an **embed code** to display your video on your website or blog — perfect for product launches or tutorials.

</Steps>

---

## 🎉 You’re All Set!

You’ve successfully created your first Cinema8 video.  
Start experimenting with more **interactive elements**, **analytics**, and **templates** to level up your storytelling and engagement.
