# Adding Subtitles to Your Video

Subtitles enhance accessibility, comprehension, and viewer retention. With **Cinema8**, you can add subtitles using manual input, file uploads, or automatic AI-powered generation.

---

## How to Add Subtitles

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Your Project

- **For new videos:** Click **New Video** on your dashboard to begin a new project.
- **For existing videos:** Go to **My Videos**, select a video, and click **Add Interactivity** to launch the Cinema8 Studio.

### Step 2: Access the Subtitles Tool

- In the Studio interface, locate the **Quick Tools** section.
- Click **Add Subtitles** to open the subtitle editor.

</Steps>

---

## Subtitle Options

### Manual Entry

- Select your desired **language** before adding subtitles.
- Type your captions directly into the editor.
- Use the **timeline** to sync text with video playback.

### Upload a Subtitle File

- Upload an existing `.SRT` file to import subtitles.
- Review and fine-tune the **timing** as needed using the timeline.

## Using AI-Generated Subtitles

If you're on the **Starter Plan** or higher, you can let AI handle subtitle creation:

- Enable **Auto-Subtitle Generation** in the Subtitle Editor.
- Cinema8 will generate captions in over **100 languages**.
- Review and adjust the subtitles for accuracy before saving.
