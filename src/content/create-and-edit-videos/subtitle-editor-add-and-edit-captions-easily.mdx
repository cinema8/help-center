## Accessing the Subtitle Editor

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Your Video

- Go to **My Videos** on your Cinema8 dashboard.
- Select a video and click **Edit Video**.

### Step 2: Launch the Subtitle Editor

- Click on **Add Subtitles** in the editor to open the Subtitle Editor interface.

</Steps>

---

## Adding Captions

### Manual Entry

- Click **Add New Caption**.
- Enter your subtitle text.
- Use the timeline to set precise **start and end times**.

### Uploading a Subtitle File

- Click **Upload Subtitle File** and select a `.SRT` or `.VTT` file.
- Once uploaded, you can preview and fine-tune the timing for accuracy.

---

## Editing Subtitles

- Click on any subtitle block to **edit text**.
- Drag or adjust the **timeline handles** to resync timing.
- Use the **Preview** function to check alignment with the video.

---

## Customizing Subtitle Appearance

- Change **font size**, **style**, and **color**.
- Adjust **background opacity** for readability.
- Choose the **position** of subtitles on the screen (e.g., bottom center, top left).
