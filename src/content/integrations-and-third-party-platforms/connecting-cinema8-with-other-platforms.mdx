# Connecting Cinema8 with Other Platforms

<Callout type="info">
Cinema8 integrations allow you to enhance your workflows by connecting to third-party platforms like analytics tools, content embeds, and custom APIs.
</Callout>

Cinema8 offers seamless integration with various platforms to help you:

- Track performance using analytics
- Embed videos in third-party environments
- Extend workflows with API-driven automation

## How to Integrate Cinema8 with Other Platforms

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Integration Settings

Log in to your Cinema8 dashboard, navigate to **Account Settings**, and select the **Integrations** tab.

### Step 2: Choose a Platform

Cinema8 supports integrations with platforms like **Google Analytics**, **Iframely**, and custom APIs. Click on the platform you want to configure.

### Step 3: Complete Required Fields

Follow the instructions for the selected platform. This might include adding an API key, inserting a tracking code, or configuring an iframe URL.

### Step 4: Test the Integration

Verify everything is working by checking the data flow (e.g., events appearing in Google Analytics) or ensuring embedded content loads correctly.

</Steps>

<Callout type="important">
Each platform may have its own specific setup steps. Visit the [Cinema8 Knowledge Base](/) for detailed guides on supported integrations.
</Callout>
