# Exporting SCORM Packages from Cinema8

<Callout type="info">
SCORM allows Cinema8 videos to be embedded into popular LMS platforms while supporting detailed learner tracking and completion rules.
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open Sharing Options

Open your video in **Cinema8 Creative Studio**. Click the **Share** button in the top-right corner and navigate to the **SCORM Export** tab.

### Step 2: Configure SCORM Settings

- **Select a SCORM version**:
  - *SCORM 1.2*: Compatible with most LMS platforms, including Moodle, Blackboard, and Akademi.net.
  - *SCORM 2004 (3rd Edition)*: Offers extended tracking and reporting.
- **Set a minimum watch time**: Define how much of the video must be watched for the LMS to register completion.
- Complete all required fields and save your configuration.

### Step 3: Export the SCORM Package

Click **Export** to generate a ZIP file. Download and store the SCORM package on your device.

### Step 4: Upload to Your LMS

Go to your LMS (e.g., Akademi.net or Moodle) and upload the package via the Content Upload or Course Manager. Then:
- Configure **completion criteria**
- Enable **progress tracking**
- Assign the content to a course or lesson

</Steps>

<Callout type="important">
Akademi.net supports both SCORM 1.2 and SCORM 2004, ensuring full compatibility with Cinema8 exports.
</Callout>

## Why Use SCORM with Cinema8?

- **Standardized integration** with e-learning platforms
- **Track learner progress** and completions
- **Combine LMS and Cinema8 analytics** for deeper insights

## Best Practices

- Set appropriate watch time requirements for fair learner assessment
- Test the package after upload to ensure reporting works as expected
- Use Cinema8's analytics alongside LMS data for more robust reporting
