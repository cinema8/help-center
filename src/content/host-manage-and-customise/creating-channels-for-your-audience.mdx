# Creating Channels for Your Audience

Cinema8 **channels** allow you to curate and present your video content in a branded, audience-facing environment. Channels can be used for training hubs, product showcases, customer onboarding portals, and more.

---

## How to Create a Channel

<Steps>

### Step 1: Access the Channel Manager

- Navigate to **My Channels** from the Cinema8 dashboard.

### Step 2: Add a New Channel

- Click **Add New Channel** to open the creation form.
- Complete the required fields under:

  - **Basic Information**
    - Channel name (e.g. "Customer Training Hub")
    - Channel handle (custom URL slug)
    - Description (what the channel is about)

  - **Branding**
    - Upload a **profile picture**
    - Upload a **banner image** to display at the top of the channel

### Step 3: Save and Populate the Channel

- Click **Save** to create the channel.
- You can now start adding **videos** and **playlists** to build your channel content.

</Steps>
